An Introduction to the ECO4 Scheme

Lower Bills, Warmer Homes, and a Greener Future

The ECO4 Scheme is a UK government initiative designed to improve the energy efficiency of homes across the nation.

Understanding the Benefits of ECO4

ECO4 offers a compelling combination of advantages. By making your home more energy-efficient, you can significantly reduce your energy bills, leading to long-term financial savings.
Who Can Participate in ECO4?
Eligibility for ECO4 is determined by several factors, including your income level, property type, and energy performance certificate (EPC) rating. The scheme prioritizes low-income households and those experiencing fuel poverty.

Transforming Your Home

A Look at ECO4's Energy Efficiency Improvements

ECO4 tackles energy waste in your home with a multi-pronged approach. They focus on improvements that directly impact your energy consumption, like:

Targeted Insulation

ECO4 targets heat loss with loft & cavity wall insulation, draught-proofing for year-round comfort & lower energy bills.

Heating System Makeover

ECO4 offers modern, energy-saving boiler upgrades for lower bills and a cozier home.

Renewable Energy Integration

Some ECO4 schemes offer solar panels for sun-powered electricity, reducing reliance and promoting sustainability.

Ongoing Efficiency Measures

ECO4 goes beyond upgrades with ongoing energy-saving advice for a lasting impact.

Is Your Home Eligible for ECO4 Support?

Unlock a more comfortable and energy-efficient home with ECO4! This government-backed scheme offers a variety of benefits to help homeowners and tenants across the UK reduce their energy bills and contribute to a greener future. While eligibility criteria exist, the program is designed to be inclusive, so there's a good chance your home could qualify. Here are four key factors to consider

Property Type

ECO4 is generally available for owner-occupied homes and some private rented properties.

Energy Performance Certificate (EPC) Rating

Homes with lower EPC ratings (typically E, F, or G) are prioritized as they stand to benefit most from energy-saving improvements.

Household Income

The scheme often targets low-income households and those experiencing fuel poverty. However, ECO4 support may also be available to some higher-income households depending on the specific scheme and installer.

Benefits Received

If you are currently receiving certain government benefits, such as Income Support or Universal Credit, you may be eligible for ECO4 support regardless of your income level.

Location

While ECO4 is available throughout the UK, some areas may have additional eligibility criteria or specific types of ECO4 measures offered due to local factors. You can check with your local council or participating ECO4 installers for any location-specific details.

To determine definitively if your home qualifies for ECO4, simply use the quick and easy online eligibility checker. In just a few minutes, you can discover the potential for a more comfortable, energy-efficient, and affordable home.
